Accessibility for Web

About 15% internet-users in the World have disability: visual, physical or hearing disability. How to make website with accessibility for all of them?

American Workforce Rehabilitation Act (section 508) obliges us to think about people with disability but there are many situations when people without disability need it too. Let's take a closer look at how we can fix this.

Situation Decision
We can't use a mouse

There is a simple, but strong rule: you have to add tabindex for all interactive elements on your page.

Don't forget about icons and logo if they are lead to change page.
We have small screen/we can't see It's very important to set alt tag for images. Write "beautiful girl on the beach near sea" instead of "girl.jpg". Set aria-label tag for links that were change on icons.
Too small font

It's not a good way to save space and put more information to the page.

Take another look on the page. You have at least two option.

First: concise text: delete not important clarification and sentence that are inaccurate.

Second: if you can't do first, just add option "Read more" for users who are interested in.

Too many scripts,

scripts insted of links

Start with HTML-code. Using right tags according with newest specification can solve many problems.

JavaScript-code is an addition on the base of HTML.
Inability to rotate screen

Try to think about concept that will make your application or web-site is available for most commonly used types of laptops, tablets and smartphones without rotating.
Slow internet connection

Try to divide information on the page on main and additional.

What is the most important information for your user? Not only text.

It may be a picture how to put on respirator, for example.

Main information shouldn't weigh a lot to load quickly even if the internet signal is weak.
Autofill is off

Imagine situation when something went wrong, and all fields that you've been filling in is gone.

Autofill is a good choise for all situations exept personal data and identity information.
